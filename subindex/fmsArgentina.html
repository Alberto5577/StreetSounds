FMS ARGENTINA

The 4 classified to FMS International

FMS Argentina has reached Matchday 4 and that means that the qualifiers for FMS International have already been defined.

NÚCLEO WILL REPLACE TATA ON THE JURY

Núcleo will replace Tata on the FMS Argentina jury after the national champion's decision to rededicate himself to battles.

DAY CONFIRMED FOR DATE 5

A couple of days before the fourth date, the organization confirmed that on Sunday, December 13, the fifth day of FMS Argentina will be held. There are no confirmed matches yet, but there is a lot of enthusiasm for the date that will mark the middle of the season.

THE MVP'S UNTIL THE FOURTH DAY

Without a doubt, the Argentine league is one of those that stands out the most among the other four, not only because of its competitiveness, but also because of the characteristic flow of the MCs and because of the aggressiveness in their way of rapping.
